How Strategic Marketing and Personalized Creative Increased App Installs

Overview

Ola Cabs, an India-based transportation app, partnered with AdParlor to launch their ride-hailing services into the UK market. With a goal to generate cost- efficient App Installs across several UK city launches, AdParlor mixed hands-on media management and agile creative services to drive success.

Strategy

Highlighting Ola’s brand, AdParlor designed customizable ads to promote awareness in new markets. Each ad featured adjustable elements that were quickly tailored per demographic, geographic location, promotion, and holiday via the Creative Blueprints technology. Using a variety of different creatives allowed Ola to test and discover what creative resonated best with each audience.

With a thorough understanding of both Ola’s service and audience, AdParlor developed a multi-channel, go-to-market strategy for driving App Installs. Pairing customized targeting with customized creative, Facebook App Install campaigns – along with Google Universal App Campaigns and Apple Search – were leveraged to promote demand-side installs.
